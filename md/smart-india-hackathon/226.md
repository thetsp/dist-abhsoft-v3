**Problem Statement Title:** Automatic Drug Dispenser

**Description:** Develop an Automatic Drug Dispenser that uses QR codes in prescriptions to dispense the correct medicines efficiently, reducing long queues and improving patient convenience.

**Domain:** Healthcare, MedTech/BioTech/HealthTech

**Solution Proposal:**

**Resources Needed:**
- Healthcare Professionals
- Software Developers
- Hardware Engineers
- Pharmacists
- Regulatory Experts
- Manufacturing Facilities
- Funding and Investment

**Timeframe:**
- Research and Planning: 6-12 months
- Hardware and Software Development: 12-18 months
- Regulatory Approvals: 6-12 months
- Manufacturing and Deployment: 12-24 months
- Pilot Testing and Scaling: Ongoing

**Technology and Material Requirements:**
- QR Code Scanners
- Pharmacy Database Software
- Drug Dispensing Mechanisms
- Secure Cloud Storage
- Pharmacy Management Software

**Team Size:**
- Healthcare Professionals: 2-3 members
- Software Developers: 5-6 members
- Hardware Engineers: 2-3 members
- Pharmacists: 2-3 members
- Regulatory Experts: 1-2 members

**Scope:**
1. Research and Planning: Identify the most efficient hardware and software solutions, assess regulatory requirements, and plan deployment.
2. Hardware and Software Development: Create a user-friendly QR code-based system for efficient drug dispensing.
3. Regulatory Approvals: Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations and obtain necessary approvals.
4. Manufacturing and Deployment: Establish manufacturing facilities and deploy the dispensers in hospitals and pharmacies.
5. Pilot Testing and Scaling: Conduct pilot tests, gather feedback, and scale the solution to more healthcare facilities.

**Learnings:**
- Regulatory compliance in the healthcare sector.
- Integration of hardware and software in a medical device.
- Healthcare supply chain management.
- Patient feedback and continuous improvement.

**Strategy/Plan:**
1. Research and Planning: Collaborate with healthcare professionals and regulatory experts to understand requirements and ensure compliance.
2. Hardware and Software Development: Develop a robust QR code-based drug dispensing system with user-friendly interfaces.
3. Regulatory Approvals: Work closely with regulatory bodies to meet all healthcare standards.
4. Manufacturing and Deployment: Establish manufacturing facilities and partner with hospitals and pharmacies for deployment.
5. Pilot Testing and Scaling: Launch pilot programs, gather data on efficiency and safety, and scale the solution based on feedback.
